Improve the process of starting a live streaming for the OA&KOL streamer

Based on a field user research on “Starting A Live Streaming by Shopee Live Official Account”, we discovered some functional issues and captured the design hints through observing the user behavior and having a follow-up interview with them. Considering the actual priority arrangement of the product features, we firstly solved two issues: a. Enable the streamer to customize the amount of coins rewards, and can continuously reward coins during the live streaming; b. simplify the process of adding mass products into a stream and presenting them in a specific period.
